ABOUT ME

Allow me to introduce myself

I have always been amazed at computers ever since the day I was introduced to it at school. I can still remember the first DOS based apps I used and the games I played. At that point, I knew clearly what I would do for the rest of my life. Since then I have strived to learn and practice what I can in developing software.

After graduating from college, I worked as a programmer developing WinForms applications. There I have been exposed with various business solutions including but not limited to ERP, CRM, Accounting, Stock Management, and eCommerce just to mention a few.

My current specialty is mostly on developing server-side APIs and database/server management. Also, with my ever increasing passion for web technologies, I strive to always be updated and versed with it. I do a lot of studying in order to maintain and/or improve my skillset as a full-stack web developer.

When I'm not developing apps or software, I'm probably with my family, hanging out with friends or watching movies or TV series. I also like being inspired by reading or watching new technology updates and trends. I also like playing online games (DOTA2 / Diablo 3).
